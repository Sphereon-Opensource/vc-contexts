Terminology

initialApproval

The date and time on which the the organization had its initial ISO certification

issueDate

The date and time on which the certificate was issued.

validUntil

The date and time after certificate is no longer valid.

certificateNo

The unique ISO certificate Number.

standardId

The ISO standard the certificate is issued for. Example: ISO 9001:2015

organizationName

The name of the organization that received the certificate
