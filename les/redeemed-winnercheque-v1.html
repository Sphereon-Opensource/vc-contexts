Terminology

wcChequeId

Unique identifier belonging to the cheque.

wcCryptoAddress

The crypto address of the recipient.

wcMonetaryAmount

The amount that should be transferred to the crypto address.

wcMonetaryCurrency

The currency of the amount that should be transferred to the wallet.

wcMonetaryAmountInWords

The amount in words to correspond with monetaryAmount.

wcRecipientName

The name of the recipient.

wcDepositAddress

The wallet address of the recipient.

transactionId

The transaction id of the redemption transaction.

redemptionDate

Date / DateTime of when the winner cheque has been redeemed.
