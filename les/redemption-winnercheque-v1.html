Terminology

redemptionTransactionId

The transaction id of transfer stable coins from player to casino for the redemption transaction.

redemptionDate

Date / DateTime of when the winner cheque has been redeemed.

lesWinnerCheque

The winner cheque that has been redeemed.
  • chequeId
    Unique identifier belonging to the cheque.
  • monetaryAmount
    The fiat amount that should be transferred.
  • monetaryCurrency
    The fiat currency of the amount that should be transferred.
  • cryptoAmount
    The amount that should be transferred to the crypto address.
  • cryptoCurrency
    The currency of the amount that should be transferred to the crypto address.
  • monetaryAmountInWords
    The amount in words to correspond with monetaryAmount.
  • transactionId
    The transaction id of transfer stable coins from casino to player during creating winner cheque.
  • player
    The player for winner cheque.
    • playerId
      Unique identifier belonging to the player.
    • name
      The name of the player.
    • middleName
      The middle name of the player.
    • surname
      The surname of the player.
    • dateOfBirth
      The date of birth of the player.
    • cryptoAssets
      The crypto asset type and address collection of the player.
      • asset
        The asset type of the player crypto address. e.g. USDC, USDT
      • cryptoAddress
        The crypto address of the player for the asset.
